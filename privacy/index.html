Privacy Policy

How we handle information

This page explains how Huanelia Studio approaches privacy for its mobile apps, projects, and related services.

Huanelia Studio

Independent software studio based in Singapore, building mobile apps for reflection, productivity, and everyday organization.

Last updated: May 17, 2026

Overview

Huanelia Studio develops mobile applications for reflection, productivity, progress tracking, subscription tracking, and everyday organization.

We aim to build products that are useful, respectful of users' time, and mindful of personal information.

Information We Collect

Our apps may store user-provided content such as journal entries, progress records, app preferences, subscription details, or other information entered by the user.

Where possible, this information is stored locally on the user's device.

How We Use Information

Information is used to provide app functionality, improve the user experience, and support features requested by the user.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data.

If an app uses third-party services for features such as payments, analytics, cloud services, or artificial intelligence functionality, those services are used only to support app functionality.

Data Storage

Some app data may be stored locally on the user's device. Future features may use cloud services where required to support app functionality.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at support@huaneliastudio.com.
