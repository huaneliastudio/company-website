Growth Journal

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Growth Journal handles information when you use the app.

Last updated: 6 June 2026

1. Overview

Growth Journal is a progress journaling app developed by Huanelia Studio. The app is designed to help users record, organize, and review personal progress across areas such as work, reading, learning, routines, and self-reflection.

We value user privacy and aim to keep the app simple, transparent, and respectful of personal information.

2. Information You Enter Into the App

When using Growth Journal, you may choose to enter information such as:

  • Work logs, project notes, and task descriptions
  • Reading notes, book titles, quotes, summaries, and key ideas
  • Learning notes, topics, takeaways, and application ideas
  • Dates, tags, mood selections, and progress categories
  • Blockers, next steps, reflections, and personal notes
  • Other information you choose to add to your progress entries

This information is used to provide the app's core features, including progress tracking, journaling, search, filtering, entry review, and personal insights.

3. Local Storage

Growth Journal is designed to store progress entries and related information on your device unless otherwise stated. This helps you manage your records directly within the app.

If future versions introduce cloud sync, account features, analytics, artificial intelligence features, or other online services, this Privacy Policy will be updated to explain how those features work and what information may be processed.

4. Notifications

Growth Journal may request permission to send notifications if reminder features are offered. Notification permission is controlled by your device settings, and you can enable or disable notifications at any time.

Reminder notifications are intended as helpful prompts. Delivery may depend on device settings, system limitations, app permissions, and other factors outside our control.

5. Purchases and Premium Features

If Growth Journal offers paid features, subscriptions, free trials, or lifetime purchases, payments are processed by Apple through the App Store. Huanelia Studio does not directly collect or store your full payment card details.

Apple may process payment-related information according to Apple's own terms and privacy policies.

6. Information We Do Not Sell

We do not sell your personal information.

We do not use progress entries, journals, reading notes, learning notes, or work logs that you create in Growth Journal for advertising sales or data brokerage.

7. Analytics, Advertising, and Third-Party Services

The current intended design of Growth Journal does not rely on third-party advertising. If analytics, crash reporting, cloud services, artificial intelligence services, or other third-party tools are added in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated to describe those services.

8. Data Accuracy and User Control

You are responsible for the information you enter into Growth Journal, including its accuracy, completeness, and timeliness.

You may add, edit, review, or delete your progress records within the app where those features are available.

9. Data Deletion

You can delete information that you entered into the app by using the app's available delete or edit features. You may also remove the app from your device. Depending on your device settings and backup configuration, copies of app data may remain in device backups controlled by your operating system or Apple account.

10. Sensitive Personal Content

Because Growth Journal allows users to write personal notes and reflections, you should avoid entering information that you do not want stored on your device. You are responsible for the content you choose to record in the app.

11. Children's Privacy

Growth Journal is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us.

12. Security

We take reasonable steps to design Growth Journal in a privacy-conscious manner. However, no app, device, or electronic storage method can be guaranteed to be completely secure. You are responsible for keeping your device, Apple ID, and device passcode secure.

13. International Users

Growth Journal may be available to users in different countries or regions. By using the app, you understand that your use of the app may be subject to the laws and platform rules applicable in your location.

14. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we may update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of Growth Journal after changes are posted means you accept the updated Privacy Policy.

15. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or Growth Journal's privacy practices, please contact us at:
