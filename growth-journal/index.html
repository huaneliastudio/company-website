Growth Journal

Track what you do, read, learn, and improve.

Growth Journal is a journaling and progress tracking app designed to help users record daily work, reading, learning, and personal growth in a structured way. It helps users reflect on their progress, organize their thoughts, and build a clearer picture of their development over time, with iCloud sync available across Apple devices when enabled.

Available Soon on the App Store Contact Support

Designed for simple progress tracking.

Growth Journal focuses on helping users capture meaningful daily progress across different areas of life without making the process feel complicated.

Work Logs

Record work progress, projects, tasks, and what was completed during the day.

Reading Progress

Track books, reading notes, key ideas, quotes, and reflections from what you read.

Learning Notes

Capture topics, lessons, applications, and useful takeaways from learning sessions.

Journal History

Review past entries, filter by type, search records, and revisit progress over time.

Progress Insights

Review recent progress, open next steps, focus areas, recurring themes, and monthly patterns.

iCloud Sync

Keep entries available across Apple devices when iCloud is enabled, while your journal remains private to your Apple account.

Privacy

Built with privacy in mind.

Growth Journal stores journal entries on device and can sync them across Apple devices using the user's private iCloud account when iCloud is enabled.

The app does not require a separate account and does not operate a custom server for journal data.

Progress Plus

Progress Plus is a one-time lifetime unlock for users who want deeper progress reviews, next-step tracking, focus areas, recurring themes, and Markdown export.

Guided Progress Review

Review momentum, follow-through, patterns, and priority next steps from recent logs.

Monthly Review

Summarize monthly activity across work, reading, and learning entries.

Next-Step Tracking

Track open and completed next steps so notes can turn into action.

Export Report

Export a Markdown progress report for saving, sharing, or reviewing outside the app.
