Personal Progress Tracker

Track what you do, read, learn, and improve.

Personal Progress Tracker is a journaling and progress tracking app designed to help users record daily work, reading, learning, and personal growth in a structured way. It helps users reflect on their progress, organize their thoughts, and build a clearer picture of their development over time.

Coming Soon on the App Store Contact Support

Designed for simple progress tracking.

Personal Progress Tracker focuses on helping users capture meaningful daily progress across different areas of life without making the process feel complicated.

Work Logs

Record work progress, projects, tasks, and what was completed during the day.

Reading Progress

Track books, reading notes, key ideas, quotes, and reflections from what you read.

Learning Notes

Capture topics, lessons, applications, and useful takeaways from learning sessions.

Journal History

Review past entries, filter by type, search records, and revisit progress over time.

Progress Insights

Identify recurring themes and patterns from past entries to better understand personal growth.

Local-First Experience

Designed to keep progress tracking simple, private, and focused on the user's own records.

Privacy

Built with privacy in mind.

Personal Progress Tracker is designed to help users record and organize personal progress information clearly and responsibly.

The app focuses on practical journaling, progress tracking, and reflection while keeping the experience simple and easy to understand.
