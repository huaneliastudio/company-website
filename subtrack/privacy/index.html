SubTrack+

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how SubTrack+ handles information when you use the app.

Last updated: 22 May 2026

1. Overview

SubTrack+ is a subscription tracking app developed by Huanelia Studio. The app is designed to help users record, organize, and review their own subscription information, including renewal dates, prices, billing cycles, categories, and reminders.

We value user privacy and aim to keep the app simple, transparent, and respectful of personal information.

2. Information You Enter Into the App

When using SubTrack+, you may choose to enter information such as:

  • Subscription names
  • Subscription prices
  • Billing cycles, such as monthly or yearly
  • Renewal dates
  • Categories
  • Reminder preferences
  • Optional notes or other details you choose to add

This information is used to provide the core features of the app, including subscription tracking, spending overview, and renewal reminders.

3. Local Storage

SubTrack+ is designed to store subscription tracking information on your device unless otherwise stated. This helps you manage your subscriptions directly within the app.

If future versions introduce cloud sync, account features, analytics, or other online services, this Privacy Policy will be updated to explain how those features work and what information may be processed.

4. Notifications and Reminders

SubTrack+ may request permission to send notifications so that it can remind you about upcoming subscription renewals. Notification permission is controlled by your device settings, and you can enable or disable notifications at any time.

Reminder notifications are intended as helpful prompts. Delivery may depend on device settings, system limitations, app permissions, and other factors outside our control.

5. Purchases and Subscriptions

If SubTrack+ offers paid features, subscriptions, free trials, or lifetime purchases, payments are processed by Apple through the App Store. Huanelia Studio does not directly collect or store your full payment card details.

Apple may process payment-related information according to Apple's own terms and privacy policies.

6. Information We Do Not Sell

We do not sell your personal information.

We do not use subscription entries that you create in SubTrack+ for advertising sales or data brokerage.

7. Analytics, Advertising, and Third-Party Services

The current intended design of SubTrack+ does not rely on third-party advertising. If analytics, crash reporting, cloud services, or other third-party tools are added in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated to describe those services.

8. Data Accuracy and User Control

You are responsible for the accuracy of the information you enter into SubTrack+. You may add, edit, or delete your subscription records within the app where those features are available.

9. Data Deletion

You can delete information that you entered into the app by using the app's available delete or edit features. You may also remove the app from your device. Depending on your device settings and backup configuration, copies of app data may remain in device backups controlled by your operating system or Apple account.

10. Children's Privacy

SubTrack+ is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us.

11. Security

We take reasonable steps to design SubTrack+ in a privacy-conscious manner. However, no app, device, or electronic storage method can be guaranteed to be completely secure. You are responsible for keeping your device, Apple ID, and device passcode secure.

12. International Users

SubTrack+ may be available to users in different countries or regions. By using the app, you understand that your use of the app may be subject to the laws and platform rules applicable in your location.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we may update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of SubTrack+ after changes are posted means you accept the updated Privacy Policy.

14. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or SubTrack+'s privacy practices, please contact us at:
